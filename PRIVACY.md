# Hush Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 4, 2026

Hush is a simple paid sleep sounds app. It does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data.

## Data Collection

Hush does not collect personal information, analytics, advertising identifiers, location, contacts, photos, microphone input, or browsing activity.

The app stores a few preferences locally on your device, such as volume, the last selected sound, whether you have seen the timer hint, and review-prompt timing. This information stays on your device and is not sent to the developer or any third party.

## Tracking And Advertising

Hush does not include ads and does not track you across apps or websites.

## Third-Party Services

Hush does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external servers.

The app uses Apple system frameworks for audio playback, lock screen media controls, and Apple's native App Store review prompt.

## Common Questions

### Does Hush have ads or subscriptions?

No. Hush is a paid upfront app with no ads, no subscriptions, and no premium upgrade.

### Does Hush collect data?

No. Hush does not collect personal data, analytics, or advertising identifiers. Preferences are stored locally on your device.

### Does audio keep playing when the phone locks?

Yes. Hush supports background audio so sleep sounds can continue while your iPhone is locked.
